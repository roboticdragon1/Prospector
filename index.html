Claims load for the area on screen when you're zoomed in past ~town level. Pan, then tap ⟳ Claims to refresh. Source: BLM Mineral & Land Records System (MLRS) — the same official data The Diggings uses. Squares cover the claim's PLSS area and may be larger than the actual claim.

Two ways a real claim can look missing. The squares show active claims only. A historic mine that's been closed (e.g. the "Rainbow Lode" USGS record) won't show as a square — but the ground may still be claimed under a different owner's name. And a small number of BLM cases have no mapped boundary at all, so they appear in no polygon layer. When it matters, verify the exact spot 👇
